Perseverance

Mars Mission 2020

NASA's Mars 2020 Perseverance rover is all set to launch aboard the United Launch Alliance Atlas V rocket from Cape Canaveral Air Force Station in Florida on Thursday (July 30). Perseverance is designed to search for astrobiological evidence of ancient microbial life on Mars and will land in Jezero Crater on February 18, 2021.
